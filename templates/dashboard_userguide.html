Hi, our system just checked your page out and we observed some few things you should take care of:

Provide A Profile Picture & Cover Photo

The profile picture & cover photo are key aspects of a Facebook page. They make the first visual impression on users.

Provide A UserName

Usernames help to easily promote a business, brand or organization's presence on Facebook with a shortened Page address.

Provide An About Us Statement

The About description gives visitors information about the page and what to expect if they choose to follow it on Facebook.

Provide A Call To Action Button

Call to Action buttons are designed to bring a page's most important objective to the forefront and to improve user interactions.

Provide Contact Information

Contact information is very important as it allows users to get in touch with the page or easily find a way to get more information.

Provide A Milestone Strategy

Milestones are a great way of telling the story of a company or organization. It makes your brand seem friendly and relatable.
